# CSE 110 Team 11 Team Contract

This team contract is established for CSE 110 Team 11, serving as a foundational document to guide our collaboration, ensure clear communication, and promote effective teamwork throughout the project. All team members agree to abide by the following rules and commitments:

## Core Values

Team members will strive to put in fair effort into tasks, and have good faith cooperation for the betterment of the team as a whole. Honest communication of work expectations and issues that arise is crucial, as well as ensuring professional conduct.

## Team Leadership

Team Leads: Yuting and Arav

Responsibilities: Yuting and Arav will take turns hosting team meetings and sending meeting announcements. This ensures consistent organization of team discussions and timely dissemination of important information to all members.

## Slack Communication Guidelines

All team members must check Slack messages within 24 hours of receiving them. If a member fails to stay on top of Slack communications (i.e., does not respond within the 24-hour window), other team leaders may initiate a call to follow up and ensure information is received.

## Task Completion Notification

Upon completing assigned tasks, the responsible team member(s) must notify all members in the team’s Slack channel immediately. This keeps the entire team informed of project progress and avoids duplication of work or delays in subsequent tasks.

## Deadline Management

All team members are expected to meet the agreed-upon deadlines for their assigned tasks. If a member anticipates being unable to meet a deadline, they must communicate this to the team (via Slack or meeting) as soon as possible. This allows the team to adjust plans, reallocate tasks if needed, and minimize the impact on the overall project timeline.

## Meeting Attendance

All team members must arrive at scheduled meetings on time. Punctuality ensures that meetings are productive, time is used efficiently, and all members have the opportunity to participate fully in discussions.

## Communication Etiquette

All team members will communicate with one another with mutual respect. This includes listening to others’ opinions, speaking courteously, avoiding interruptions, and being open to constructive feedback. Disagreements should be addressed calmly and professionally, focusing on finding solutions that benefit the team and the project.

## Review & Update

This team contract will be reviewed regularly throughout the project lifecycle. Any updates or revisions to the contract must be discussed and agreed upon by all team members.

## Team Member Acknowledgment

By agreeing to this team contract, all team members confirm their understanding and commitment to abiding by the above rules and responsibilities.

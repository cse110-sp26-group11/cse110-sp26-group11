# Team Font & PowerPoint Theme

## Recommended Fonts

| Usage | Font Suggestion | Style |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| Headings | **Poppins** or **Montserrat** | Bold, modern |
| Body Text | **Open Sans** or **Lato** | Clean, readable |
| Accent | **Bebas Neue** (for large titles) | Strong, impactful |

---

## PowerPoint Theme

- **Primary Color:** Blue  
- **Secondary Color:** Bronze  
- **Accent:** White or light gray  
- **Logo Placement:** Top left or centered on title slides  
- **Background:** Clean white or subtle gradient (blue to bronze)
